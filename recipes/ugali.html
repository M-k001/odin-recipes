UGALI MAYAI


UGALI MAYAI

These is an African staple food,
cherished by many especially univercity comrades whom can't go without it in a span of a day or two

INGREDIENTS

  • Maize flour (unga ya ugali)
  • Water
  • Salt (optional)
  • Cooking oil
  • Eggs (2–3)
  • onion, chopped 1
  • tomato, chopped 1
  • Fresh dhania (coriander), chopped

PREPARATION STEPS

  1. Boil about 2 cups of water in a sufuria.
  2. Once boiling, gradually add maize flour while stirring with a mwiko (cooking stick).
  3. Continue stirring and adding flour until the mixture thickens to your preferred consistency.
  4. Cover and let it cook for 3–5 minutes on low heat.
  5. Stir one last time, then shape the ugali and set it aside on a plate.
  6. In a separate pan, heat a tablespoon of oil.
  7. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  8. Add chopped tomatoes and green pepper; cook until soft.
  9. Crack the eggs into the pan and stir to scramble, or fry them whole depending on preference.
  10. Season with salt, black pepper, and optional chili.
  11. Sprinkle fresh dhania and cook until the eggs are fully done.
  12. Serve the mayai hot alongside your ugali.
